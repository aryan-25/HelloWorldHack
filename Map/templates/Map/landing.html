A platform that connects humanity

Our NGO "WeCare" has been working on child nutrition for years.


Our aim is to provide nutritional food and supplements in the underpriviledged areas to reduce the rate of children dying due to malnutrition.


We recently created our profile on VolunteerED, and it was quite overwhelming to see such a great response and support from people all over the world.


We have now started getting donations from the world's top most nutrition based production companies and our services have thus increased to a great extent.

Our Vision

It is clear that Covid-19 is an unfolding humanitarian crisis that will affect all countries. Self-isolation and social distancing have given way to lockdown in many countries, with gatherings prevented and extensive restrictions on movement.


Joining hands with some of the most influential philanthropists and their movements is the key to rebuild the feeling of togetherness!


If you are looking to channel the hidden global citizen, you are at the right place!


For the volunteers, VolunteerED is the one-stop platform to help you reach out to some of the most influential activists around the world and collaborate with them on their movement. On the other hand, this is the most accessible forum for the organisations to seek the right people that match their purpose.


We will liaise with some of the notable organisations like United Nations, Malala Fun, Bill and Melinda Gates foundation and Bloomberg Philanthropies among many others. The prominent figures behind these notable foundations believe taking a social responsibility gives today’s youth a competitive advantage.


“Philanthropy gives us a competitive advantage, we think, in recruiting and retaining talent. And I can tell you from personal experience it is also good for the bottom line, as good a thing a company can do.” — Michael R. Bloomberg

Remember, it’s never too late (or early) to sign up and kick-off your journey to “Make a dent in the Universe”.
